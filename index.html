MINECRAFT 2D

How to Play:

  • Axe (🪓) removes Wood & Leaves
  • Pickaxe (⛏️) removes Stone, Ores & Snow
  • Shovel (🧹) removes Grass, Dirt & Sand
  • Sword (⚔️) fights Enemies!
  • Apple (🍎) drops from Leaves (50% chance) — click or press E to eat and heal a heart
  • Bow (🏹) shoots arrows at Enemies!
  • Bucket (🪣) collects & places water — evaporates in Desert, freezes in Snow!
  • Left Click near your player to break or place blocks
  • Green glow = correct tool  |  Red glow = wrong tool
